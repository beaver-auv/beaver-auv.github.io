About Us

We are Beaver Country Day School's dedicated Robosub team. Our team spends all year working to build an autonomous underwater vehicle to compete in the National Robosub Competition. BeaverAUV was founded in 2015 and since then, each new generation of students has added, modified and improved upon the vehicle. This team has been to 4 competitions competing in several events that range from torpedo shooting to navigating gates and we have continued to improve every year. The last time we competed in 2019, the BeaverAUV team made it to the finals for the first time in our existence and ended up taking the eighth rank against many other collegiate teams. This accomplishment greatly exceeded our wildest expectations and has forced us to set even higher goals for the future.
