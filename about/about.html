About Us


We are Beaver Country Day School's dedicated Robosub team. Our team spends all year working to build an autonomous underwater vehicle to compete in the National Robosub Competition. Beaver AUV was founded in 2015 and and since then each new generation of students has added, modified and improved upon the vehicle. This team has been to 4 competitions competing in several events that range from torpedo shooting to navigating gates and we have continued to improve every year. Last year The Beaver AUV team made it to the finals for the first time in our existence and ended up taking the eighth rank against hundreds of other teams. This accomplishment greatly exceed our wildest expectations and has forced us to set even higher goals for the future.
